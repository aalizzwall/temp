### **知覺過程記憶法則：**
1. **感官接收** - 外來刺激→神經感受器  
2. **轉導 (Transduction)** - 物理信號→神經衝動  
3. **傳遞** - 感覺傳入徑至大腦皮質  
4. **解碼** - 認知處理、記憶對比  

#### [Image Prompt: Sensory perception process flowchart showing stimulus reception by sensory receptors, transduction into electrical impulses via receptor potentials, transmission through ascending sensory pathways to thalamus and somatosensory cortex, cortical interpretation with memory integration, and clinical conditions affecting perception including anosognosia and agnosia]

### **知覺障礙分類：**
| 類型 | 定義 | 例子 |
|------|------|------|
| **幻覺** | 無刺激卻有感知 | 聽見聲音、看到影像 |
| **錯覺** | 誤解真實刺激 | 把繩索看成蛇 |
| **空間定向障礙** | 無法判斷方位 | 偏癱患者不知道左右 |
